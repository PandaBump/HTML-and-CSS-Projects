Best Peanut Butter & Jelly

For this, you will need:

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • A Butterknife
  • Optional ‑ A flat surface

Instructions

  1. Place both bread slices face down,
    on a flat surface.
  2. Use a butterknife or some utensil
    to spread the peanut butter on one slice.
    Use as much peanut butter to your taste.
  3. Using the same utensil, spread some
    of the jelly that you chose on the other
    slice of bread.
    Again, using as much jelly as you
    would prefer.
  4. Take one slice of bread and place
    it on the other, ensuring both the peanut
    butter and jelly sides are together.
  5. Enjoy a delicious peanut butter
    & jelly sandwich!
